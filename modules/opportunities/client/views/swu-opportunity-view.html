Proposal Evaluation Criteria

Here's how your proposal will be scored:

Your Team's Capabilities & Skills 10 points

In order for your company to be considered, the team members you put forward must collectively possess all the required capabilities and be available for the time periods stated.

In the proposal, you may also see a list of preferred skills. The more of these your team has, the higher they'll score.
The Questionnaire 20 points

All companies' responses to the questionnaire get anonymized and randomized. Then the issuer has to rank them from strongest to weakest. Once they decide how many companies they want to interview, they get to see which companies scored the highest and screen through to Interview.

Whether your team screens through for an Interview depends on the strength of responses and the number of companies that apply. Either way, we'll let you know right away.
The Interview 60 points
Pricing10 points

Your score for pricing is based on an algorithm that considers your bid compared with the the lowest and highest bids.
