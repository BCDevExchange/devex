Register Your Company on the BCDevExchange
Registering your company means that any site viewer will be able to see:
  • Your company name
  • A link to your website if you choose to share it
  • A list of the Sprint With Us capabilities your company possesses by virtue of its members*

*The individual members you associate to your company profile will NOT be seen by site viewers; they will only be seen by the issuer of a government opportunity to which you've put those members forward in a proposal.
