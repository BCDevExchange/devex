In an effort to make it faster and easier for government and developers to work together, the BCDevExchange has created two new procurement processes.

Code With Us drives government to develop its digital products in the open and enables government to instantly reach a large community of talented software developers and quickly acquire enhancements to government digital products.

To date over $700,000 has been paid out to developers under small opportunities ranging from $1,000 - $70,000, many of whom have never worked for government.

Sprint With Us enables government to hire an entire software development team within 30 calendar days for contracts of up to 2 years or $2M. Sprint With Us allows teams to demonstrate their strength through a code challenge and in-person scenario. And under a new Agile Software Development Agreement, teams get to bring their innovation to shape the product, rather than being locked into delivering on a pre-determined set of requirements.

Come learn about these new opportunities and how you can apply. If you’ve already applied for opportunities or participated in the process, we’d be keen to get your feedback and learn how we might improve the process.
