The Government of B.C. is procuring Agile software development teams.

If your company wins a Sprint With Us opportunity, here's how you can expect to work with us:

Government Product Managers

You'll work closely with a trained government product manager who has the expertise and the responsibility to run the service.

Agile

You'll be adaptable to change and follow the Agile Scrum process, building and validating features iteratively with users.

Interdisciplinary Teams

Your team will possess all the skills necessary for continuous delivery, such as DevOps engineering, front-end and back-end development, and user experience research and design.
