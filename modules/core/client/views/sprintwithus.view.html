Sprint With Us    How to Apply

The Government of B.C. is ready to work in a new way to deliver software products.

Sprint With Us is our new procurement model to contract companies like yours to supply Agile software development teams.

If your company wins a Sprint With Us opportunity, here's how you can expect to work with us:

Government Product Managers

You'll work closely with a trained government product manager who has the expertise and the responsibility to run the service.

Agile

We'll be adaptable to change and follow the Agile Scrum process, building and validating features iteratively with users.

Interdisciplinary Teams

Our team will be a maximum of nine people and possess all the skills necessary for continuous delivery, such as Scrum Master, software development, and UI/UX design and research.
