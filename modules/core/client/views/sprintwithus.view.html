Sprint With Us  

The Government of B.C. is ready to work in a new way to deliver software products.

Sprint With Us is our new procurement model to contract companies like yours to supply Agile software development teams.

If your company wins a Sprint With Us opportunity, here's how you can expect to work with us:

Government Product Owners

You'll work closely with a trained government product owner who has the expertise and the responsibility to run the service.

Agile

We'll be adaptable to change and follow the Agile Scrum process, building and validating features iteratively with users.

Interdisciplinary Teams

Our team will be a maximum of nine people and possess all the skills necessary for continuous delivery, such as scrum master, software development, and UI/UX design and research.

Agile Phases

Don’t expect lengthy requirements. This is Agile. We'll start with Inception to understand the business problem. Then we'll build a Proof of Concept to demonstrate the feasibility of our solution. Finally, we'll build out the rest of the product in Implementation.

Open Source

Your team will own the code that's produced, but it will be published in GitHub under an open source license.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to qualify for and apply on Sprint With Us opportunities:

1. Sign In & Claim Capabilities

  1. Sign in to the BCDevExchange with your GitHub account
  2. Claim the capabilities you possess in the My Capabilities & Skills tab in your BCDevExchange Profile

2. Register & Qualify Your Company

Register

To register your company:

  1. Make sure you're signed in
  2. Go to the Companies page and click on the + Register a Company button

Qualify

To qualify, your company needs a team who combined have all of these capabilities. Invite the people you want on your team to join the BCDevExchange and claim their capabilities (see Step 1).

Make sure they tell you the emails they used in their profiles so you can add them to your team.

From the Members & Capabilities tab in your company profile:

  1. Add members to your company
  2. Once you can see that all of the capabilities are covered off, read and agree to the qualification terms

3. Submit a Proposal

Once you’ve qualified, find an opportunity you want to bid on and submit a proposal:

  • Make sure you're signed in
  • Find an opportunity
  • Click on the Submit a Proposal button to begin

The proposal is a lightweight process where you:

  • Agree to the Request for Proposal Terms & Conditions, and any additional terms and conditions if applicable
  • Select the members of your company you want to assign to each of the Agile Phases in the opportunity
  • Answer 5 questions (1,000 word limit per question) that tells us about your company
  • Submit a price for your team for each of the Agile Phases


Help Us Improve

This is a new procurement model that we’ve designed and are testing with real opportunities. We want to make procurement easier and the delivery of software continuous. We expect to learn and iterate, so please share your feedback on the process!
