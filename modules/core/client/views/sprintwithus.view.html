Sprint With Us

The Government of B.C. is ready to work in a new way to deliver software products.

Sprint With Us is our new procurement model to contract for Agile software development teams from the tech sector.

If your company is successful through the Sprint With Us process, here's how you'll work with us:

Agile

Teams will be adaptable to change and follow the Agile Scrum process, building and validating features iteratively with users.

Interdisciplinary Teams

Teams will be a maximum of nine people and possess skills such as scrum master, software development, continuous delivery, and UI/UX design and research.

Government Product Owners

Teams will work closely with a trained government product owner with the expertise and the responsibility to run the service.

Agile Phases

Don’t expect lengthy requirements. This is Agile. Teams will start with Inception to understand the business problem. Next they will build a Proof of Concept to demonstrate the feasibility of their solution. Finally, they will build out the rest of the product in Implementation.

Open Source

Code produced will be owned by the contracted team, but published in GitHub under an open source license.

How to Apply

First, you need to register your company and ensure it qualifies for our list of Sprint With Us companies. To qualify for Sprint With Us opportunities your company needs all of the Agile capabilities. These capabilities are linked to the people who work for your company.

Qualify

Follow these simple steps to qualify:

  1. If you don't have a GitHub account, Sign up here.
  2. Sign in to the BCDevExchange with your GitHub account.
  3. Ask members of your team to sign in to the BCDevExchange and edit their profiles, making sure to check off the capabilities and include their email.
  4. Register your company.
  5. In your company profile, add members of your team (who have signed up to the BCDevExchange) by entering their emails (the ones they used in their profiles).
  6. Once you have all the capabilities required to qualify for Sprint With Us, read and agree to the qualification terms.

Submit a Proposal

Once you’ve qualified, you can bid on open Sprint With Us opportunities, following these simple steps:

  • Find an opportunity you want to bid on
  • Make sure you've signed in, registered your company, and have all of the required capabilities.
  • Submit a proposal
  • Agree to the contract for the work
  • Select the members of your team you will assign to each of the Agile Phases in the opportunity. Make sure they have all the capabilities required for each Phase in the opportunity!
  • Answer 5 questions (1,000 word limit per question) that tells us about your company
  • Your bid price for your team for each of the Agile Phases


Help Us Improve

This is a new procurement model that we’ve designed and are testing with real opportunities. We want to make procurement easier and the delivery of software continuous. We expect to learn and iterate, so please share your feedback on the process!
