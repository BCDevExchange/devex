How to use this website to respond to a Sprint With Us Request for Proposals

Follow these steps to use this website to respond to the Sprint With Us Request for Qualifications and respond to a Request for Proposals:

1. Sign In & Claim Capabilities

  1. Sign in to the BCDevExchange with your GitHub account
  2. Claim the capabilities you possess in the My Capabilities & Skills tab in your BCDevExchange profile

2. Register & Qualify Your Company

Register

To register your company:

  1. Make sure you're signed in
  2. Go to the Companies page and click on the + Register a Company button

Qualify

To qualify, your company needs to be able to provide a team that has all of these capabilities. You will need to ensure that you have invited each of the team members to join the BCDevExchange and that they have claimed their capabilities in their BCDevExchange profile (see Step 1).

Make sure they tell you the emails they used in their profiles so you can add them to your team.

From the Members & Capabilities tab in your company profile:

  1. Add members to your company
  2. Once you can see that all of the capabilities are covered off, read and agree to the qualification terms

The proposal is a lightweight process where you:

  • Agree to the Request for Proposal Terms & Conditions, and any additional terms and conditions if applicable
  • Select the members of your company you want to assign to each of the Agile Phases in the opportunity
  • Answer 5 questions (1,000 word limit per question) that tells us about your company
  • Submit a price for your team for each of the Agile Phases
  • Include references

What happens after you respond to the Request for Proposals

Here's what happens after you submit your proposal through the website.

Code Challenge

Depending on your proposal score, your team may be screened in for a Code Challenge. Either way, you'll be notified promptly whether your team is in the running.

A maximum of 4 teams will move to the Code Challenge.

Teams will be given 5 (five) business days to complete the Code Challenge in GitHub. The challenge will be evaluated by two independent evaluators and a static code evaluator. Those who complete the Code Challenge and meet the evaluation requirements will then proceed to the Team Scenario.

Team Scenario

Teams that successfully complete the Code Challenge will be invited to participate in a 3-hour Team Scenario.

At the start of the meeting, your team will be presented with a scenario where they can demonstrate their capabilities and how they work as a team under pressure. This will be followed by questions of your team from the evaluation panel, including follow up questions on both the scenario and coding challenge.

Your entire team should participate in the Team Scenario. This can be arranged either in person or by video conference.

Contract

The scores for all phases will be totalled and the highest scoring team will be awarded the contract.
