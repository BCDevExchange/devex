 5. Code Challenge & Team Scenario

About the Code Challenge & Team Scenario

Shortlisting

Based on their cumulative scores for steps a) and b), only the top four ranked Proponents (the “Shortlisted Proponents”) will then proceed to the next stage of the RFP process. The Shortlisted Proponents will be notified that they will be eligible to participate in the code challenge set out in step d) below. The notification will explain the nature of the code challenge.

Code Challenge

This step consists of a Code Challenge, which will be provided on GitHub, providing the Proponents with 5 (five) business days to complete. The code will be evaluated according to criteria assessing Proponent’s:

  1. approach to the problem;
  2. knowledge of the technology;
  3. functionality of the app;
  4. code quality;
  5. code maintainability; and
  6. code organization.

Upon being notified of being shortlisted for this Code Challenge step, shortlisted proponents will receive the applicable evaluation criteria and other instructions regarding this step.

Only those Proponents that have met the minimum score for above step d) will be eligible to participate in the Team Scenario described in step e) below.

Failure to meet the minimum score will mean that the Proponent’s proposal will receive no further consideration and the Proponent will be eliminated from the competition.

Team Scenario

Shortlisted Proponents, who have progressed to this step will be notified of the date, time and place of the exercise. At the exercise, Proponents will be provided with a scenario designed to permit Proponents to demonstrate their capabilities and demonstrate how they work as a team under pressure.  Qualities to be evaluated will be leadership, teamwork and approach. This will be followed by questions for the Proponent from the evaluation committee, including follow up questions for both the scenario and the Coding Challenge. The Team scenario can be facilitated in person or by video conference.

Evaluation – General Terms
  1. Evaluation of proposals will be by a committee formed by the Province and may include the Product Manager, employees and contractors of the Province and other appropriate participants and stakeholders.
